### The Manufacturing of Consent

Modern propaganda techniques:

1. **Framing**: How stories are presented matters more than facts
2. **Omission**: What's not reported is as important as what is
3. **Source bias**: "Anonymous sources" and "experts say"
4. **Repetition**: Repeat a lie enough, it becomes truth

## Spotting Media Manipulation

Protect yourself from information warfare:

- **Check ownership**: Who owns the media outlet?
- **Follow the money**: Who funds the organization?
- **Cross-reference**: Compare multiple sources
- **Question timing**: Why is this story breaking now?
- **Identify emotional manipulation**: Are they making you angry/afraid?

> **Critical Thinking**: The most powerful weapon against propaganda is your ability to think independently.
